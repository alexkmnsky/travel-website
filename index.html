Places We Visit:

Opera House:

Visit one of the most distinctive and famous buildings of the 20th century and experience one of the many art performances!

Harbor Bridge:

The Sydney Harbour Bridge is one of Australia’s most iconic landmarks. Stunning views of the Sydney harbour are visible from the bridge.

Darling Harbor:

Drop by one of many of the attractions at the harbour including restaurants, the Harbourside Shopping Centre and tours.

Royal Botanic Gardens:

30-hectare botanical garden established in 1816, home to a collection of plants from Australia and the world.

Bondi Beach:

Australia’s most famous beach, home to one of the oldest lifesaving clubs in the world. Bondi Beach has spectacular views of the ocean.

Tower Eye:

The tallest structure in Sydney. The tower has a view of the city from its observation deck.

Sydney Aquarium:

Aquarium that features a large variety of Australian aquatic life, displaying more than 700 species from most of Australia's water habitats.
